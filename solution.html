How Can We Reduce Noise Pollution?

Implement these measures to reduce noise pollution:

  • Use noise barriers or insulation in buildings.
  • Encourage the use of electric vehicles.
  • Enforce stricter noise regulations for industries.
  • Promote awareness about the harmful effects of noise pollution.
