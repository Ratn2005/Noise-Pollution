What is Noise Pollution?

Noise pollution refers to unwanted or harmful sounds that interfere with normal activities or disrupt the quality of life. It can cause stress, hearing loss, and other health problems.

Effects and Consequences

  • Hearing impairment
  • Stress and anxiety
  • Sleep disturbances
  • Cardiovascular issues

Statistics

According to the WHO, around 1.1 billion teenagers and young adults are at risk of hearing loss due to exposure to noise pollution from recreational activities.
